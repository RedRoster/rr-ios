RedRoster is a social course roster for Cornell University.

Do you have a Cornell University email? Students who sign in with their email can see the schedules and reviews created by other students.

• Create public and private schedules
• View your friends' schedules
• View schedules in both list and calendar format
• Read course reviews by other students and write your own
• See who else is taking a course with you
• View courses offline that you've already looked at

This app is not sponsored by Cornell University. Please use the official Cornell course roster for academic planning.
